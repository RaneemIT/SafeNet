Our Lessons

Introduction to Cybersecurity

Teacher: Dr.Sara

Category: Cybersecurity Fundamentals

This chapter introduces the basics of information security, explaining its importance in protecting data and systems from cyberattacks. It covers key concepts such as the CIA triad (confidentiality, integrity, availability), types of attackers, basic attack steps, and fundamental security principles used to defend systems..

Types of Malware

Teacher: Dr.Reem

Category: Malware & Threats

explains malware, which is malicious software that enters a system without the user’s knowledge. It introduces different types of malware such as viruses, worms, botnets, spyware, keyloggers, rootkits, and ransomware, and explains how they spread, hide, or steal information from systems.

Encryption

Teacher: Eng.Ahmed

Category: Cryptography Basics

This chapter introduces symmetric-key encryption, where the same secret key is used for both encryption and decryption. It explains traditional symmetric ciphers such as substitution ciphers and transposition ciphers, which form the basic foundation of modern cryptography.
